## Platform Optimization

**TikTok:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- 15-60 seconds
- Fast cuts (every 2-3 seconds)
- Trending audio when possible
- Text overlays (large, easy to read)
- Captions (many watch muted)

**Instagram Reels:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- 15-90 seconds
- Trending audio boosts reach
- Cover frame matters (like thumbnail)
- Use transitions
- End with question/CTA

**YouTube Shorts:**
- 9:16 aspect ratio
- <60 seconds
- First frame = thumbnail (text + face)
- Hook in first 1 second
- Captions/subtitles
- Subscribe CTA at end
